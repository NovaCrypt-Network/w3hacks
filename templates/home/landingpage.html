About w3Hacks

w3Hacks is an online platform where anyone can learn how to code! Whether you're a seasoned programmer or a semi-beginner, everyone has something to learn here! We reinforce and teach good, real-world programming skills that are used in the software engineering industry, and crucial skills for any developer to know whether they're making a solo project or working with others!

Why w3Hacks?

Real-World Preparation

We provide real-world exercises that help you build skills that are used heavily in the software engineering industry. w3Hacks is the only resource that teachings industry-level skills, and we give tons resources to help you learn those skills.

Virtual Hackathons

We allow our users to express their creativity, as well as showcase their abilities through virtual hackathons. We consistently hold virtual hackathons where all of our users can participate in, no matter the skill level or age of the competitor.

Help On-Demand

Programming is hard. So that's why we provide help and guidance on demand. We have a Discord server that anyone can join filled with other users and mentors, and we are completely open for technical questions on your projects or specific exercises!

Friendly Competition

For those who like a little competition, we rank our users by the amount of exercises they complete and hackathons they win, but as well as the quality of their projects/exercises. You are also rewarded credits for hackathons and completed exercises!
